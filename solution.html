How JalRakshak fixes real problems

Our Solution

JalRakshak is a complete smart water management system designed to solve real problems faced by homes, schools, hospitals and villages.

It replaces manual water control with intelligent automation. Once installed, it works independently without human supervision.

Core Capabilities

JalRakshak continuously monitors water level using ultrasonic sensors. It ensures tanks never overflow and never run dry.

Flow sensors detect hidden leakages inside pipelines. This helps prevent water loss and structural damage.

Turbidity sensors monitor water quality. If water becomes unsafe, the system alerts users immediately.

Why JalRakshak is Different

Most smart systems depend on internet and expensive hardware. JalRakshak works fully offline and is affordable for common people.

It is designed especially for Indian conditions where internet is unreliable and water scarcity is critical.

Real Impact

JalRakshak can save thousands of litres of water every month, reduce electricity bills, increase pump lifespan, and improve public health.
