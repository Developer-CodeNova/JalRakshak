JalRakshak

Smart Water Tank Health Monitoring & Protection System

JalRakshak is an intelligent smart system designed to automatically manage water tanks, prevent water wastage, detect leakage, protect pumps from damage, and ensure safe water for human health.

It is built specially for real Indian conditions where internet is unreliable, budgets are limited, and water scarcity is a serious issue.

Why JalRakshak?
